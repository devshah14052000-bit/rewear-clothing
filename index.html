Welcome to ReWear

ReWear is an online marketplace where students can buy, sell and exchange pre-owned clothing. Our goal is to make fashion affordable while reducing clothing waste.

The Problem

Fast fashion creates waste and students often struggle to afford trendy clothing. Many clothes remain unused in wardrobes.

Our Solution

ReWear allows students to sell and buy second-hand clothes easily through an online platform.

How It Works

Create an account, upload clothing photos, set prices, and connect with buyers.

Clothing Categories

  • Casual Wear
  • Streetwear
  • Formal Clothing
  • Sportswear
  • Vintage Fashion

About Us

ReWear is a student startup focused on sustainable and affordable fashion.
