Affordable Sustainable Fashion

Welcome to ReWear

ReWear is an online platform where students can buy, sell, and exchange second-hand clothing. Our goal is to make fashion affordable while reducing clothing waste.

The Problem

Fast fashion produces massive waste. Many clothes are worn only a few times before being discarded. Students also struggle to afford trendy clothing.

Our Solution

ReWear provides a marketplace where users can list their unused clothes and buy affordable fashion from other students.
